About Maredia Enterprise

Engineered for Durability

Maredia Enterprise Industries is a precision metal stamping and forming manufacturer serving the automotive and two-wheeler sector since 2006. We supply OEM-grade sheet metal body parts direct to assembly lines, distributors, and specialized workshops worldwide.

Our 45,000 sq.ft. facility houses 14 progressive die stamping lines, robotic welding cells, and a full powder coating and electroplating line enabling us to deliver complete body kit solutions from a single controlled environment.

  • Full-service stamping, forming, welding, and surface treatment
  • Custom tooling and die development for OEM programs
  • Low MOQ sampling with high-volume production scalability
  • 3D scanning and CMM verification on all production lots

How We Make It

Manufacturing Process

Every part follows a rigorous 5-stage production workflow. From concept engineering to final dimensional verification, we maintain full traceability and quality records at every step.

Download Process Deck
01
Phase One

Design & Engineering

CAD-based part design using CATIA and SolidWorks. DFM analysis ensures optimal material utilization, draw depth, and springback correction before any tooling investment.

CATIA V5 SolidWorks FEA Simulation DFM Review
02
Phase Two

Tool & Die Fabrication

Precision hardened steel dies manufactured on CNC 5-axis machining centers. Progressive, transfer, and blanking dies produced in-house with typical T1 lead time of 4560 days.

5-Axis CNC EDM Wire Cut Tool Steel D2 Tryout & Debug
03
Phase Three

Stamping & Forming

Press capacities from 63T to 1000T across 14 stamping lines. Progressive die operations achieve multi-feature parts in a single press stroke, minimizing handling and variation.

631000T Press Progressive Die Deep Drawing Robotic Transfer
04
Phase Four

Finishing & Treatment

Full in-house surface treatment: zinc phosphating, E-coat priming, powder coating, and electroplating. Color matching and gloss level controlled to OEM specifications.

Phosphating E-Coat Powder Coat Electroplate
05
Phase Five

Inspection & Dispatch

100% visual inspection on critical appearance surfaces. Dimensional verification by CMM and 3D scanning for first-off and periodic audits. Full SPC documentation provided.

CMM Verification 3D Scanning 100% Visual SPC Reports
